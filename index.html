Hao ZHANG （张浩）

About Me

I am currently an assistant professor at Hunan University.

I received my PhD in September 2020 from IMJ-PRG under the supervision of Pierre Charollois and Henri Darmon. Here is my thesis.

My research interests are on modular forms, L-functions, elliptic cocycle, Hecke operators.
